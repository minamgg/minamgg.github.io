Research Focus

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at Nanyang Technological University.

My research investigates maritime AI socialisation, autonomous-driving trust and anthropomorphic design, Bayesian-network and system-dynamics modelling of ship and port systems, green shipping policy, and VR-enabled marine conservation.

My future work focuses on human-swarm teaming in high-risk maritime environments, cognitive load management, interface transparency, and multi-agent collaboration mechanisms for intelligent maritime systems.
